## 2. What Petwell does

Petwell is a pet-health organizer and educational companion. Depending on your plan and settings, it can help you:

- **Triage symptoms.** Answer a few short questions — starting with safety — and Petwell shares "possible causes" to consider and a sense of how urgent things may be. This is educational, not a diagnosis.
- **Keep records.** Build pet profiles and log food, stool, skin, weight, activity, medications, symptoms, and more — your pet's history in one place.
- **Get reminders.** Set care reminders so routine tasks and medications are easier to keep up with.
- **Check food (food intelligence).** Look up or scan foods to review ingredients and surface things worth knowing, with honest, non-pay-to-rank information.
- **Prepare vet-ready summaries.** Turn your logs into a clear summary you can bring to or share with your veterinarian.

Optional **AI features** (such as assistant chat, explanations, label reading, and record summaries) are off by default and, when enabled, are informational only — not veterinary advice. AI can be wrong or incomplete, and AI-extracted data is unverified until you review it. Confirm anything important with your veterinarian. See our Privacy Policy for how AI features handle your data.

Features evolve over time, and some may be limited by your platform, region, or plan.
